Peanut Noodles

Yummy and not too naughty!

You can go from door to dinner in under 20 minutes with these noodles, perfect for a speedy meal after a long day. Filling and delicious they won't leave you feeling guilty. This recipe from Nora Cooks is the ideal post work quick fix.

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles - 28g
  • Edamame - 1 cup
  • Vegetables
    • Cucumber - 1 cup chopped
    • Carrots - 1 cup grated
    • Red bell pepper - 1 thinly sliced
    • Onions - 2 chopped
    • Coriander - 1/4 cup chopped
  • Chopped peanuts 1/4 cup chopped

  • Peanut Sauce
    • Natural peanut butter - 1/2 cup
    • Soy Sauce - 1/2 cup
    • Garlic - 2 cloves, crushed
    • Lime juice - 2 tbsp
    • Agarve - 3 tbsp
    • Hot Sauce - 1 tspn
    • Water - 1/2 cup

Steps

  1. Whisk peanut sauce ingredients together in a small bowl.
  2. Cook noodles, drain and add to a large bowl.
  3. Add all veggies, edamame and peanuts to the bowl along with the peanut sauce. Toss to combine and serve with a sprinkle of red chili flakes or sesame seeds and more coriander, if desired.
